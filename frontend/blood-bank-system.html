Donor Eligibility Checker
Eligibility Criteria Reference
✅ Age: 18 – 65 years
✅ Weight: Minimum 50 kg
✅ Hemoglobin: ≥ 12.5 g/dL
✅ Blood Pressure: Systolic 90–160, Diastolic 60–100 mmHg
✅ Last Donation: ≥ 90 days ago
❌ Illness: No illness in past 2 weeks
❌ Pregnancy: Not currently pregnant
❌ Blood Thinners: Not on anticoagulants
❌ Tattoo: No tattoo within 6 months
Eligible donors can donate once every 90 days. Each donation is typically 1 unit (450 mL).
